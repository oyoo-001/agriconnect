About AgriConnect

Built by people who know the land

We started AgriConnect because too many good harvests never made it to the right buyer. Today we're a marketplace built to close that gap — for farmers, consumers, agro-vets, and the industries that depend on them.

We believe a farmer's fair price shouldn't depend on how many middlemen stand between the field and the market. Our job is to shorten that distance.

AgriConnect exists to give every farmer, regardless of the size of their plot, direct access to buyers who want what they grow. No opaque pricing, no unpaid invoices, no guesswork about who's actually buying.

On the other side, consumers, agro-vets, and industry buyers get a transparent way to source fresh, verified products — with payments held safely in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

We're a small team, but we grew up around farms, cooperatives, and local markets, and we built the platform we wish had existed for the people we know.

Our story

How AgriConnect came to be

A short history of the decisions that shaped the platform.

2023

The idea takes root

After watching local farmers lose margin to unreliable middlemen, our founding team started sketching a direct farmer-to-buyer marketplace on a whiteboard in a rented co-working space.

2024

First pilot with 40 farmers

We launched a manual, WhatsApp-and-spreadsheet pilot with 40 farmers in two counties, just to prove that buyers would pay a fair price when given transparency and a reliable delivery promise.

2025

Escrow and OTP delivery confirmation

We built the secure escrow system that still powers every transaction today, so payment only releases once a buyer confirms delivery with a one-time code.

2026

Platform opens to everyone

AgriConnect went public, now connecting over 1,200 farmers with thousands of consumers, agro-vets, and industry buyers across the region — with more joining every week.

What we stand for

The principles behind the platform

Every feature we build gets measured against these four commitments.

Fair by default

Pricing and commissions are visible before anyone commits to a transaction. No hidden cuts, no surprise deductions.

Trust is earned in escrow

Every payment is held until delivery is confirmed, protecting both sides of a trade equally.

Built for the field, not the desk

Most of our users manage listings from a phone in the middle of a workday, so mobile comes first in everything we design.

Community over competition

Farmers can share advice, request products, and support each other through the community forum — growth here isn't zero-sum.

Answer fast, fix faster

Real-time notifications and a responsive support team mean issues get resolved in hours, not days.

Grow with the ecosystem

We build for the whole value chain — farmers, consumers, agro-vets, and industry — because each one makes the others stronger.
