How AgriConnect works

Four steps connect the entire agricultural value chain — from farm to table, seamlessly and securely.

01

Farmers list products

Upload crops, livestock, dairy, or produce with photos, prices, and delivery details in minutes.

02

Consumers browse & buy

Search fresh farm products by location, category, or price. Purchase securely with escrow protection.

03

Secure transactions

Payments held in escrow until delivery is confirmed via OTP verification for buyer protection.

04

Earn and grow

Farmers get paid, the platform earns commission, everyone benefits from a transparent marketplace.
