Getting Started

Click "Get Started" on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email. You can register as a Farmer, Consumer, Agro-Vet, or Organization.
Farmers list produce, Consumers shop, Agro-Vets supply inputs, and Organizations/Industries source raw materials. Each role has tailored features.
Yes, registration is free. AgriConnect earns a small commission on each successful transaction to sustain the platform.
Yes, you can update your profile or add additional roles from your Account Settings at any time.

Buying & Orders

Browse product listings, click "Order" on an item, specify quantity, and confirm. The seller will receive your request and can accept or negotiate.
Payments are processed securely through the platform. Funds are held in escrow until the order is confirmed delivered and both parties are satisfied.
Yes, you can cancel before the seller confirms. After confirmation, contact the seller or our support team to resolve.
Visit the Orders section in your dashboard to see real-time status updates from "Pending" through to "Delivered".

Selling & Commissions

Log in as a Farmer, go to "Post Product" in the sidebar, fill in the details (name, price, category, images), and submit. Your listing goes live instantly.
A small percentage of each transaction is retained as a platform fee. The exact rate is displayed before you confirm any sale.
Earnings are deposited into your AgriConnect wallet. You can withdraw to your bank account or mobile money at any time.
Yes, you can edit or delete your listings anytime from the "My Products" section in your dashboard.
